How this is calculated

  • Percentiles are computed across the listed jobs using each job's midpoint (the average of its advertised low and high), converted to the selected currency. This follows the methodology of the Horizon Salary Benchmarking sheet.
  • Typical advertised range shows the median of advertised lows and the median of advertised highs — a representative posted band, since salaries are listed as ranges rather than single figures.
  • Seniority comes from each job's experience field. Junior = 1–4y, Mid = 5–9y, Senior = 10y+. A role open to several levels is counted in each, so the "All roles" total is not the sum of the rows.
  • Filters combine with AND across categories and OR within a category (e.g. selecting two skills matches jobs with either skill).
  • Exchange rates are fixed values from the Horizon sheet.
