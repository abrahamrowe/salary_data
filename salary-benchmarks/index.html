How this is calculated

  • Percentiles are computed across the listed jobs using each job's midpoint (the average of its advertised low and high), converted to the selected currency.
  • Typical advertised range shows the median of advertised lows and the median of advertised highs — a representative posted band, since salaries are listed as ranges rather than single figures.
  • Seniority is taken from each job's experience level: Junior = 1–4y, Mid = 5–9y, Senior = 10y+. A role open to several levels is counted in each, so the "All roles" total is not the sum of the rows.
  • Filters combine with AND across categories and OR within a category (e.g. selecting two skills matches jobs with either skill).
  • Exchange rates are mid-market rates.
